About

The study and analysis of online media content can be complex and time-consuming. Content is increasingly scattered across multiple sources, due to the rise of easy to use online publishing tools and social media.

The MediaViz platform aims to assist in better understanding the dynamics, trends, and patterns of this large media archive. The system continuously collects the content published by a set of user-defined online sources, persists it in a database, and makes it available through an API. The data provided by the API is consumed by a set of interactive visualization tools, allowing users to explore and analyze it.

Project funded by SAPO Labs/U. Porto.

Tools

Chronicle

The Chronicle tool displays which topics have been or currently are under the media's spotlight, and how their coverage changes throughout time and according the nature of the sources selected.

Flow

The Flow tool allows users to visualize and compare article publication and share count patterns of online sources according to distinct temporal granularities.

NewsMap

The NewsMap allows users to visualize a source's (or group of sources) geographic coverage, both at the international and national level. Users can also input text to find co-occurrences of the introduced search term and the countries’ or Portuguese subdivisions’ names.
